03It polices itself

Three independent quality layers, because a small model's confidence can't be trusted alone.

①

Confidence gate

Cheap per-item triage. Fails open to Claude on blank, malformed, or truncated output — never crashes the batch.

②

Spot-check audit

Samples the batch independent of confidence and judges it by the Wilson upper bound on error — a clean tiny sample is honestly flagged "audit more," not rubber-stamped.

③

Claude escalation

Only the genuinely hard cases reach the frontier model. In the demo the workers nailed the clear papers and the audit caught the subtle boundary calls.

05Two applications, one toolkit

📄 Research brief assembly line

Abstracts in, a polished evidence brief out — filtered, summarized, scored, and quality-audited entirely by local workers.

🔬 Autoscience deep-research loop

A keep-or-revert research loop where a local model proposes each move and scores a construct-fit metric — Karpathy-style autoscience on modest hardware.

06Why it matters

At ten abstracts the savings are a rounding error. At thousands of documents across many projects, the bulk work moving from a frontier bill to a modest electricity bill is the whole game — and the frontier model still does the part only it can: design the chain, judge the hard calls, and write the result.
